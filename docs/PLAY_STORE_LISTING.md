Last updated: April 5, 2026

## App Name

CurveCue

## Short Description

Audio curve alerts and route preview for twisty roads.

## Full Description

CurveCue is a driving-assistance app that reads the road ahead and announces upcoming curves before you reach them.

Built for scenic drives, mountain roads, unfamiliar routes, and motorcycle touring, CurveCue combines route analysis, live position tracking, and spoken prompts to help you anticipate turns earlier.

### Key Features

- Spoken alerts for upcoming curves and turn severity
- Speed advisory estimates based on road geometry
- Route preview before starting a session
- Driving modes for car and motorcycle use
- Adjustable timing, narration verbosity, and audio behavior
- Offline region support for downloaded routing data
- On-device storage for preferences and recent destinations

### How It Works

CurveCue analyzes a route, identifies curve segments, estimates severity, and times announcements based on speed and progress. During an active session, the app uses location to determine where you are on the route and when to play the next cue.

### Important Safety Notice

CurveCue is a driving aid, not a navigation or safety system. Route analysis and speed advisories are estimates only and may be inaccurate. Always obey road conditions, traffic laws, signage, visibility limits, and your own judgment. Do not interact with the app while driving.
