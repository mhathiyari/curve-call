# CurveCue Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

CurveCue is a driving-assistance app that provides curve narration, route analysis, and related map features. This policy describes what data the app accesses, how it is used, and what choices users have.

## Who This Policy Applies To

This policy applies to the Android app `CurveCue` published by the developer listed on the Google Play store listing for the app.

## Data the App Accesses

CurveCue may access the following categories of data when features are used:

- Precise location
- Approximate location
- Search queries entered by the user for destinations
- Route and destination coordinates needed to calculate or preview a route
- App settings and recent destinations stored locally on the device
- Basic diagnostic information generated by the Android platform and libraries

## How the App Uses Data

CurveCue uses data for these purposes:

- To determine the user’s current position during an active driving session
- To time curve narration and route progress updates
- To search for destinations and display human-readable place names
- To calculate routes, including online fallback routing when offline data is unavailable
- To download and cache map tiles and region data selected by the user
- To save app preferences and recent destinations on the device

## Data Sharing and Network Services

CurveCue does not sell personal data.

When certain features are used, limited data may be sent to third-party map or routing services in order to fulfill the request:

- Destination search and reverse geocoding requests may be sent to OpenStreetMap-based Nominatim services
- Online route requests may be sent to OSRM routing services
- Map tile requests may be sent to OpenStreetMap tile servers or equivalent map tile endpoints used by the app

Those services receive only the request data needed to respond, such as search text, coordinates, and standard technical request metadata.

## Data Storage

CurveCue primarily stores data locally on the user’s device.

Local data may include:

- Driving mode and audio/settings preferences
- Disclaimer acknowledgement
- Recent destinations
- Downloaded region packages and cached map tiles

CurveCue does not currently provide user accounts, cloud backup, or server-side profile storage as part of the app experience.

## Data Retention

Most app data remains on the device until the user clears app data, uninstalls the app, or removes downloaded data from within the app where supported.

Network providers used for search, routing, or tile delivery may retain request logs according to their own policies.

## Children

CurveCue is not directed to children.

## Security

Reasonable efforts are made to reduce unnecessary data collection and to store user preferences locally when possible. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

## User Choices

Users can:

- Deny location permission, though core driving-session features will not function correctly without it
- Decline to use destination search or online routing features
- Clear app data or uninstall the app to remove locally stored preferences and recents

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as the app changes. The most current version should be published at the privacy policy URL listed on the Google Play store listing.

## Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact email listed on the app’s Google Play store listing.
